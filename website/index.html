How it works

Three steps to voice typing

01
⌥

Hold Option

Press and hold the Option key anywhere on your Mac. Speakboard starts listening instantly.

⌥ Hold to record
02
🗣️

Speak naturally

Talk at your normal pace. Our AI understands context, handles accents, and captures every word accurately.

03
✨

Release to type

Let go of the key. Your words appear instantly wherever your cursor is — emails, docs, anywhere.
